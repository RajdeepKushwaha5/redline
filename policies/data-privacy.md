# Data Privacy Policy

**Version:** 1.3
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2024
**Last Reviewed:** April 1, 2026
**Owner:** Legal Department
**Approved By:** General Counsel

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs how Acme Corp collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data. We are committed to respecting the privacy of our employees, customers, and partners in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees and contractors who handle personal data in any format.

## 3. Data Collection

We collect personal data only for legitimate business purposes. Data collected may include:
- Name, email address, phone number
- Employment information
- Financial information for billing and payment
- Usage data from our products and services

Data is collected through direct interactions, automated systems, and third-party sources.

## 4. Lawful Basis for Processing

All processing of personal data is conducted in accordance with applicable law. We rely on the following bases:
- Performance of a contract with the data subject
- Compliance with legal obligations
- Consent, where appropriate
- Legitimate business interests, balanced against data subject rights

## 5. Data Subject Rights

Acme Corp respects the rights of data subjects under applicable data protection laws. Individuals may:
- Request access to their personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of their data in certain circumstances
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@acmecorp.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

## 6. Consent

Where we rely on consent as a lawful basis, consent is obtained before data collection. Individuals may withdraw consent by contacting us at privacy@acmecorp.com.

## 7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. The following retention schedule applies:

| Data Category | Retention Period | Justification |
|---|---|---|
| Employee personal data (active) | Duration of employment + 7 years | Tax and labor law compliance |
| Employee personal data (terminated) | 7 years from termination date | Employment records, legal holds |
| Customer account data | 7 years from last transaction | Financial regulations, fraud prevention |
| Support and inquiry records | 3 years from resolution | Customer service history, legal defense |
| Marketing contact data | Until consent withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity | GDPR lawful basis verification |
| Security and access logs | 1 year | Incident investigation |
| Audit logs | 7 years | Regulatory compliance |

When a retention period expires, data will be securely deleted or anonymized. Retention periods are reviewed annually by the Legal Department.

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
- Access controls
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Regular security assessments
- Employee training on data protection

## 9. Third-Party Sharing

We may share personal data with third-party service providers who assist with our business operations. All third parties are required to protect personal data in accordance with applicable law and our contractual requirements.

## 10. Data Breach

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to individuals, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority and affected individuals as required by applicable law.

## 11. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually. Questions about this policy should be directed to privacy@acmecorp.com.
