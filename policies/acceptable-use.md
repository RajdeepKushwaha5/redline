# Acceptable Use Policy

**Version:** 2.1
**Effective Date:** January 10, 2024
**Last Reviewed:** April 1, 2026
**Owner:** IT Department
**Approved By:** CTO

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of Acme Corp technology resources. All employees and contractors must comply with this policy when using company systems, networks, and devices.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and temporary staff who use Acme Corp technology resources, including company-owned and personal devices.

## 3. General Use

- Company technology resources are provided for business use
- Limited personal use is permitted during non-work hours
- Users are responsible for the security of their credentials
- All data on company systems is the property of Acme Corp

## 4. Device Policy

### 4.1 Company Devices
- Endpoint protection software must be installed
- Devices must be encrypted
- Automatic screen lock must be enabled
- Unauthorized software must not be installed

### 4.2 Personal Devices
- Personal devices may be used to access company systems only with written approval from the employee's manager **and** the CISO
- MDM (Mobile Device Management) enrollment is required before personal device access is granted
- Company data must not be stored on personal devices outside of IT-approved applications
- Personal devices used for company access are subject to remote wipe in the event of loss, theft, or termination

## 5. Internet and Email

- Internet access is for business purposes
- Do not access illegal or inappropriate content
- Do not open suspicious emails — report them to IT
- Do not auto-forward company email to personal accounts

## 6. Software and Cloud Services

- Only IT-approved software may be installed
- Cloud storage of company data requires IT approval
- Use SSO for all services that support it

## 7. Prohibited Activities

- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Installing malicious software or hacking tools
- Sharing credentials with others
- Circumventing security controls
- Using company resources for personal commercial activities
- Sending harassing or discriminatory communications

## 8. Monitoring

Acme Corp monitors technology resource usage for security and compliance purposes. Users should have no expectation of privacy when using company resources.

## 9. Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination. Serious violations may be reported to law enforcement.

## 10. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually. Questions should be directed to it-security@acmecorp.com.
